# Strategy

---

## Current Focus

Building the foundation. Before scaling client acquisition, the priority is having a system that works — a repeatable process for diagnosing businesses, presenting findings clearly, and onboarding clients confidently. The pipeline already has warm leads; the goal is to convert them.

---

## Top 3 Priorities

1. **Build the client acquisition and diagnostic system** — A clear, repeatable process: outreach → discovery call → audit → visual ROI presentation → proposal. No code shown to clients. Everything framed around money saved, time recovered, and business impact.

2. **Convert warm pipeline to paying clients** — Four leads are waiting on demos. The immediate goal is to run those demos, show tangible value, and land the first paying client. Property developer, GP surgery owner, and travel agency owner are the active targets.

3. **Establish operational foundations** — Systems, templates, and automations that let the business run smoothly as a one-person operation: client onboarding, communication workflows, proposal templates, and follow-up sequences.

---

## What Success Looks Like

### 6 Months
- At least 2–3 paying retainer clients
- A proven audit-to-onboarding process that can be replicated
- Active LinkedIn presence generating inbound interest
- First West Africa conversation initiated
- MakeMeAI is a functioning, revenue-generating business

### 12 Months
- 5–10 active retainer clients across tiers
- International pipeline open (West Africa / Nigeria)
- At least one partnership with a complementary AI or tech business
- Systematised enough to bring in a partner or contractor for delivery
- MakeMeAI is an established consultancy, not just a startup

---

## Growth Strategy

- **LinkedIn:** Daily content + targeted connection outreach to SME owners and CEOs. Build authority as the person who makes AI simple.
- **Warm network:** Leverage existing relationships. Referrals from early clients will be the fastest growth lever.
- **Events:** Business card + QR code → landing page. In-person trust-building.
- **Demos:** Tailored, sector-specific demos that show immediate value (use /demo command).
- **Cold outreach:** Future phase — once the system is proven and capacity exists.
- **West Africa expansion:** Nigeria-first. Pricing stays consistent. Relationships built through network and targeted outreach.

---

## Key Decisions in Progress

- **Pricing for training/education services** — Workshop and AI Champion programme pricing is being defined. Current benchmarks: half-day £500–£1,500, full-day £1,500–£3,000, AI Champion monthly £500–£1,000.
- **Partnership model** — Open to technical partnerships with other AI businesses to extend delivery capability without hiring.
- **Demo format** — Working out the best way to run demos that convert sceptical, non-technical business owners.

---

## North Star Metric

**First paying retainer client.** Everything else is in service of that milestone.
