## What The Company Does

MakeMeAI Consulting Ltd is an AI agency that goes into small, medium, and large businesses to identify where they are losing money, wasting time, and operating inefficiently. The company diagnoses pain points, implements AI automation to fix them, and trains staff to use AI confidently — turning fear of AI into capability.

The business operates as a long-term AI partner, not a one-off vendor. Clients stay on retainer so that as AI evolves, MakeMeAI keeps pushing them forward. The goal for every client: a more autonomous, efficient business where people are empowered with AI rather than replaced by it.

Geographic reach is UK-first but intentionally international — with a specific focus on West Africa (Nigeria and beyond), a market with strong business activity and significant unmet demand for AI services.

## How The Business Makes Money

| Tier | Monthly Fee | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Entry | £1,000/month | Smaller businesses |

**Value-based pricing:** Where measurable savings are achieved, a 10–20% share of the saving delivered is the benchmark.

**Risk-reversal onboarding:** Free audit + first month fee (£1,000–£2,500). If the client isn't satisfied after month one, full refund. No risk entry point designed to convert sceptics.

## Services

1. **AI Audit** — In-person, online, or automated. Diagnoses where the business is leaking money or time. Presented visually with clear ROI (no code, no jargon).
2. **Automation Implementation** — Identifying and automating the highest-impact pain points.
3. **AI Training & Education** — Workshops and programmes for managers, AI champions, and all staff levels.
4. **Ongoing AI Partnership (Retainer)** — Monthly AI architect relationship. Keeps the client updated as AI evolves.
5. **AI Solution Testing & Refinement** — For businesses that already have AI tools: audit, test, and enhance existing solutions.
6. **One-Off AI OS Build** — Full implementation plus training, then handover. Client runs it themselves.
