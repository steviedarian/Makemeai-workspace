# ForeShiloh First Month Guarantee -- Standard Language

## For Proposals and Contracts

Use this language in all AIOS proposals. Adjust the specific deliverable based on the client's pain point from discovery.

---

### Short Version (for conversation / email)

"Try it for 30 days. If you don't see measurable value -- real time saved, real problems solved, real results -- I'll refund the first month in full. No questions asked, no awkwardness. I only win when you win."

---

### Proposal Version (for formal documents)

**The ForeShiloh Guarantee**

We guarantee measurable value within your first 30 days.

If the AIOS does not deliver a clear, tangible improvement to your business within the first month of going live -- whether that's hours reclaimed, leads captured, costs reduced, or decisions made faster -- we will refund your first month's payment in full. No questions asked. No forms to fill out. No hoops to jump through.

We offer this because every single client we've worked with has seen value within the first week. We're not worried about refunds. We're focused on results.

Your success is our case study. Your growth is our reputation.

---

### Verbal Close (for in-person meetings)

"Look, I know this is new technology and it's a big decision. So here's what I'll do. Pay for the first month. If at the end of that month you look at this and think 'this isn't for me' -- you get every penny back. No questions, no hard feelings. I'm only interested in working with people who genuinely see the value. And honestly? Every client I've worked with has seen it within the first week. I'm not worried. Are you?"

---

### Key Principles

1. **Always say "no questions asked"** -- removes ALL friction from the guarantee
2. **Reference speed of value delivery** -- "first week" anchors their expectation that this works fast
3. **Frame it as confidence, not desperation** -- "I'm not worried" shows strength
4. **Make it about THEM, not you** -- "Your success is our case study"
5. **Keep the window at 30 days** -- long enough to prove value, short enough to feel safe for you
6. **Never guarantee specific revenue numbers** -- guarantee "measurable value" or "tangible improvement"

### What "Measurable Value" Means (examples by pain point)

| Pain Point | Measurable Value |
|---|---|
| Admin overload | Hours per week reclaimed (quantify) |
| Missed calls/leads | Zero missed calls, leads captured automatically |
| Social media | Content created and scheduled, hours saved |
| Staff costs | Tasks automated that previously required staff time |
| Decision-making | Daily intelligence brief arriving every morning |
| Client management | CRM populated, follow-ups automated, pipeline visible |
| Invoicing/chasing | Invoices tracked, reminders sent automatically |

### Contract Clause (for legal documents)

**30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee.** If the Client determines, in the Client's sole discretion, that the Services have not delivered measurable business value within 30 calendar days of the System going live (the "Guarantee Period"), the Client may request a full refund of the first month's Service Fee by providing written notice to ForeShiloh Ltd before the expiry of the Guarantee Period. Upon receipt of such notice, ForeShiloh Ltd shall refund the applicable amount within 14 business days. This guarantee applies to the first month's Service Fee only and does not apply to any upfront build fees, third-party costs, or subsequent monthly payments.
