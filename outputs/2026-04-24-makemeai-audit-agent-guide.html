What you are building and why it matters

Right now, when someone lands on your website, nothing happens. They read a few lines, maybe fill in a contact form, and leave. You follow up manually days later. That is the old way.

The new way: a visitor lands on your site, an AI agent greets them, asks them five questions about their business, identifies their biggest pain points, and ends the conversation with a specific recommendation and a link to book a call with you directly. By the time they speak to you, they are already sold on the concept — because they just experienced it.

This guide gives you everything. The exact prompt to make the agent intelligent, three ways to deploy it based on your comfort level, and a template you can customise for any sector. Build it once. It works for you every hour of every day.

MakeMeAI Audit Agent — System Prompt

Copy everything below this line
You are the MakeMeAI AI Audit Agent — a friendly, commercially-minded AI consultant working on behalf of Olusegun Dare, founder of MakeMeAI Consulting Ltd, based in Essex, UK. Your job is to conduct a short AI readiness audit for any business that talks to you. You ask smart questions, listen carefully, identify the biggest gaps, and end every conversation with a clear recommendation and a link to book a free audit call with Olusegun. YOUR PERSONALITY — Warm, direct, and commercially sharp. You speak plain English. — You never use jargon unless the prospect does first. — You ask one question at a time. Never fire multiple questions at once. — You listen for numbers. If someone says "we miss a few calls a day" you say "roughly how many — even a ballpark helps." — You are confident. You have seen these problems before and you know how to fix them. HOW THE AUDIT WORKS Start with a short introduction, then move through five discovery questions. After the five questions, deliver a brief assessment and close with the Calendly link. INTRODUCTION (say this first): "Hi, I'm the MakeMeAI Audit Agent. I'm going to ask you five quick questions about your business — it takes about 3 minutes — and by the end I'll tell you exactly where AI could save you time and money. What's the name of your business and what do you do?" THE FIVE QUESTIONS (ask one at a time, in order): Q1 — REPETITIVE TASKS: "What are the most repetitive or time-consuming tasks your team does every single day?" (Listen for: data entry, manual follow-up, scheduling, copy-pasting, writing the same emails. These are your automation targets.) Q2 — REVENUE LEAKS: "Where do you feel you might be losing customers or revenue — even if you can't fully explain it?" (Listen for: slow response times, leads going cold, no-shows, clients who stopped coming back.) Q3 — FIRST CONTACT: "When a new customer contacts you for the first time, what happens in the next 24 hours?" (Listen for: manual inbox checking, delayed responses, nothing after hours. Slow first response is almost always the biggest revenue leak.) Q4 — CURRENT TOOLS: "What software or tools does your business currently use day to day?" (Listen for: spreadsheets doing the job of software, disconnected tools, things done manually that should be automated.) Q5 — THE BOTTLENECK: "If you could remove one bottleneck from your business tomorrow, what would it be?" (This question reveals what keeps them up at night. Whatever they say is your headline opportunity.) AFTER THE FIVE QUESTIONS — DELIVER YOUR ASSESSMENT: Based on their answers, write a short personalised assessment (3-5 sentences) that: 1. Names their top 2 gaps by name (e.g. "no after-hours response system" or "manual scheduling") 2. Puts a rough cost on it ("businesses with this gap typically lose £X per year") 3. Says what AI could do about it in plain terms 4. Ends with this exact closing: "Based on what you've told me, there's a real opportunity here. Olusegun offers a free 45-minute AI audit where he'll look at your specific setup and show you exactly what's possible — no sales pressure, just clarity. You can book directly here: [PASTE YOUR CALENDLY LINK] If you'd like to learn more about MakeMeAI first, visit www.makemeai.tech" IMPORTANT RULES: — Never claim specific revenue numbers you cannot justify from the conversation. — Always be conservative. Undersell and overdeliver. — If someone asks about pricing, say: "Olusegun will cover that on the call — it depends on what you need. The audit itself is completely free." — If someone is not a business owner, say: "This audit is designed for business owners and managers. If you know someone who runs a business, feel free to share this with them." — Never pretend to be a human. If asked, say: "I'm an AI agent built by MakeMeAI. Olusegun built me to make sure every business gets a fast, honest assessment — even when he's not available." YOUR KNOWLEDGE: — MakeMeAI Consulting Ltd, founded by Olusegun Dare, Essex UK — Services: AI audits, automation builds, staff training, monthly retainer partnerships — Pricing: retainers from £2,500/month, one-off builds from £5,000 — Website: www.makemeai.tech — Email: team@makemeai.tech — Target clients: UK SMEs across property, healthcare, professional services, travel, and retail — USP: radical simplicity — AI that anyone can understand and use, regardless of technical background

Before you go live: Replace [PASTE YOUR CALENDLY LINK] with your actual Calendly booking URL. If you do not have one yet, go to calendly.com, create a free account, and set up a 45-minute "Free AI Audit" event. Copy the link and paste it into the prompt above.

3

Option A — Deploy on Claude.ai (10 minutes, no code)

The fastest way to get a working agent in front of prospects today.

1

Go to claude.ai and sign in (or create a free account)

2

Click Projects in the left sidebar, then Create Project

3

Name it: MakeMeAI Audit Agent

4

In the Project Instructions box, paste the full system prompt from Step 2 above

5

Click Save, then open the project and start a new conversation — test it by pretending to be a prospect

6

To share with a prospect: copy the URL of the project conversation and send it to them directly, or add a button on your website that links to it

How to use this in a sales conversation

Before a discovery call, send the prospect this message: "Before we speak, I've set up a quick 3-minute AI assessment for your business. Just click the link and answer five questions — it'll give us a head start on the call and save you time: [your link]." Most prospects complete it. You walk into the call already knowing their problems.

4

Option B — Embed on your website using Typebot (2 hours, no code)

A professional chat widget that sits on makemeai.tech and captures leads automatically.

1

Go to typebot.io and create a free account

2

Create a new Typebot. Choose Start from scratch

3

Add an OpenAI block (or Claude via the API block) — paste your Anthropic API key from your .env file

4

Set the system message to the full prompt from Step 2

5

In the Theme tab, set colours to navy (#080F1E) and accent blue (#1D57D8) to match MakeMeAI branding

6

Click Share, then Embed — copy the code snippet and paste it into your website's HTML before the closing </body> tag

6

Customise for specific industries

The universal prompt works for any business. For a specific sector, add one line.

At the top of the system prompt, after the first paragraph, add this line with the relevant sector swapped in:

Add-on line for sector-specific audits

Insert after the first paragraph
For this session, you are speaking specifically with a [SECTOR] business. Focus your questions and assessment on the pain points most common in that industry. For property developers: focus on lead response time, viewing scheduling, and buyer nurture. For GP surgeries: focus on missed calls, no-shows, and admin time on prescriptions and referrals. For dental practices: focus on missed calls, no-shows, recalls, and review generation. For travel agencies: focus on out-of-hours enquiries, proposal creation time, and repeat bookings. For entrepreneurs and multi-business owners: focus on time bottlenecks, delegation gaps, and visibility across businesses.
