Then I build the tools, I get them running, and I train your team so no one is left confused. And I don't disappear after that. I stay as your AI partner."
50 to 70 seconds
The outcome. Make it real.
"My clients typically save 20 or more hours every single week within the first 90 days. That is time back for you. Time for strategy, growth, or just switching off before midnight."
70 to 90 seconds
The CTA. Direct and low friction.
"The first step is a free 30-minute discovery call. No pitch, no pressure. You just leave with a clear picture of what AI can do for your specific business. Book it right here on this page."

Production Tips

Setup

Shoot at home with a clean background. Good natural light from a window in front of you is all you need.

Camera

Look directly at the lens. Your phone propped up at eye level works perfectly. No fancy equipment needed.

Captions

Always add captions. Most people watch on mobile with the sound off. CapCut auto-generates them for free.

Autoplay

Set the video to autoplay muted with captions on. Visitors press play if they want sound.

Length

Stay under 90 seconds. Attention drops fast. Every sentence should earn its place.

Appearance

Dress how you would meet a business owner. Smart but not stiff. Be yourself.
